Exception Ambiguity

Throwing an exception requires one of two things:

  • Supertype Exception, Error, and Throwable need a message. Preferably in form of sentence, although not enforced.
  • Subtype such as IllegalStateExeption, StackOverflowError, etc. are explicit enough. Therefore, can have no message. 
throw Exception() // violation
error("No data") // success
throw StackOverflowError() // success
